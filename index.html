reveal

a hidden glyph is drawn on the grid. light the right cells to uncover it.

2 edge numbers count the lit cells in that row or column
= = linked cells share a state (both lit or both dark)
× × linked cells differ (one lit, one dark)
tap a cell to cycle: unknown → lit → dark

every glyph is solvable by logic alone — you never have to guess.
